Projects






Project 1 : Sookienova

An e-commerce app for Sookienova, a fashion designer in Accra, Ghana. It comes with a client facing shop and admin dashboard for shop owner. All built with nextjs and mongodb. It allows user to create account and keep track of their previous and current order. It is also connect to hubtel, a payment gateway to help users perform payments online seamlessly.

Project 2 : Jagahmundi

Landing page and admin dashboard for Influential Sports Betting sensation, Jajah Mundi. This app allows him to manage accounts of his followers with ease independently of social media platforms. It is built with Next.js, Schadcn UI, Turso, and Lucia Auth. Also, it comes with an Express server integrated with Puppeteer for some custom operations.

Project 3: An Campaign Tracker App

A campaign tracking app built with React and Next.js. It gives the owner the ability to see all voters and their voting status. It comes with a map to help get accurate location of the users. The map service used is mapbox. It also contains a training section that users can go and watch training videos

Project4: Agency Management System

This system is designed to assist organizations in scheduling of services between their nurses and patients. They can add nurses and maintain their profiles, contact info, availabilities, and pay rates.The system enables organizations to add referrals and track the number of patients they have referred to agencies. The system keeps track of the payers of patients as well. It tracks payer requirements and makes sure they are met when assigning a payer to a patient. Major technologies I used on this project are Next.js, Typescript, Node.js, Go.

Project5: Lamstravel Travel Booking Site

A site to help users book travels with lamstravel consult. It sends the user's details to lamstravel email and to the user's mail as well when ever a user books a travel on the site.
